Activity

I believe that having an active life is a surefire way of increasing your mental clarity and overall outlook. In this industry it's very important to be able to solve problems on the fly, too often do I see other people in this industry not put in the work on their physical health. As my father says: "Becauase of the mind, the body suffers". I believe it works both ways, because of the body the mind suffers as well.

Recovery

While I do believe in pushing yourself and staying active, the body and mind need time to recover as well. Which is why I find settling in watching a good movie or show or read a book or listening to a podcast or just sitting outside calm me down and give my mind a rest from the daily workload significantly improve performance and longevity.

Family

For me, family and recovery are not mutually exclusive. Family is very important to me, without them I wouldn't be where I am today, so I try to spend time with them when I can.

Traveling

I love traveling. I'm very fortunate to have visited many countries in Europe like Italy, Sicily and the black forest in Germany to name a few and some States here in America and I can't wait to travel to another place I haven't been to yet.
